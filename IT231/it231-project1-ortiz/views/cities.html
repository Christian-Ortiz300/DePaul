Top 5 Cities

Top Cities ranked food In the USA:

https://pixabay.com/photos/new-orleans-tram-america-usa-4008968/

#1 New Orleans

Also known as "The Big Easy", New Orleans is ranked as the top city in the USA for food. it is famous for their Creole and Cajun cuisine. We also have New Orleans to thank for Sunday brunch, which originated here in 1854, and the spread of cocktail culture.

https://pixabay.com/photos/buildings-river-city-cityscape-668616/

#2 New York City

The City that Never Sleeps is known around the world for a dining scene that's vast in both volume and diversity. with a high variety of food, a traverler can find virtually any type of food experience in the Big Apple. New York city is also home to many Michelin-starred restaurants and James Beard Award-winning chefs.

https://pixabay.com/photos/chicago-usa-building-skyscraper-5347435/

#3 Chicago

The best city in the USA (in my opinion). Home to the Chicago dog and deep dish pizza, Chicago has some of the most diverse food within their neighborhoods. From chinatown to little italy, you can find a variety of food in the windy city.

https://pixabay.com/photos/griffith-observatory-los-angeles-3897616/

#4 Los Angeles

The city of angels provides the food scene with some of the most culturally diverse food one can find in the US. From food trucks to Michelin-starred restaurants, Los Angeles has it all.

https://pixabay.com/photos/san-francisco-skyline-city-4674350/

#5 San Francisco

San Francisco is known for their innovation in food. From the creation of the fortune cookie to the invention of the Irish coffee, San Francisco has a rich history in food.
